Welcome to Stable & Thyme

About us..

Sable & Thyme was founded out of love, and it's more than just a restaurant. Whether you're here for a slow dinner with friends or a quiet moment to yourself, we want every visit to feel intentional and memorable.

Our menu is a celebration of the seasons, with dishes that highlight the best of what each time of year has to offer. We take pride in sourcing local ingredients and crafting meals that nourish both body and soul. At Sable & Thyme, we believe great food should feel both familiar and surprising. Rooted in tradition but inspired by creativity, our kitchen blends bold, seasonal ingredients with thoughtful technique — crafting dishes that are as comforting as they are inventive.

Sable & Thyme — reflects our values. Sable, for richness and grounding. Thyme, for warmth, healing, and flavor that lingers. Together, they speak to what we offer: soulful food crafted with intention. We believe that food tells a story. That’s why our menu changes with the seasons, celebrating the freshest ingredients from local farms and producers. Every dish is a thoughtful balance of comfort and creativity, honoring both tradition and innovation.

We invite you to join us at Sable & Thyme, where every meal is an experience, and every visit feels like coming home. Whether you're here for a special occasion or just a quiet moment to yourself, we hope you'll leave feeling nourished in body and spirit.
